  3. What Intros Does

    Intros allows Users to track introductions they make primarily by e-mail and later request and receive feedback on these introductions, from the people they introduced. To track or record an introduction Users either

    1. BCC or CC an email address (typically my@intros.to),
    2. Forward an intro they have already made to an email address or
    3. Add an introduction manually from their account.

    Users can also record an introduction that they have received from someone else, by forwarding this email to my@intros.to. Once an introduction is tracked, Users can request feedback on the introduction from either or both of the people they introduced - via an email request sent from Intros. They can send a request manually from their account or they can set automatic feedback requests.

    A person who receives a feedback request can rate an introduction or leave feedback through a bespoke link they receive in the e-mail request.

    Intros does not contact the people who are BCC’d in an introduction.
