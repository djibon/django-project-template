WHAT'S NEW IN BRIEF

  • You can now track and follow up on intros you receive as easily as those you make.
  • There's no need to leave your email client to interact with Intros.
  • More and better insights into the key people in your network and a brand new dashboard.

GRATITUDE FOR INTROS YOU'RE GIVEN

Now, by tracking the intros you're given - as well as those you give - you'll never forget to thank those who generously help you with intros.

Why is this so important?

Most people say thanks when they receive an intro. But by the time most intros have led to something amazing, almost everyone simply forgets to tell the very person who made it possible. Finding out randomly that something cool has happened drives connectors crazy. There’s no faster way to lose goodwill. If you do follow up six months later, and share what’s happened because of a kind intro, people are insanely surprised and delighted. There’s no faster way to build goodwill.

So next time you receive an intro, simply forward it to my@intros.to.

We’ll send you a reminder in 10 days, and again in 6 months, to follow up and say thanks. You can turn these reminders on or off at any time.

DO MORE FROM YOUR EMAIL

Again and again, we've heard that stories are the most important part of assessing how an intro has developed. And hearing these stories is a great way to engage and build more meaningful relationships with the people you connected. So, we built a new system that is all based on email.

This is how it works:

1. We'll remind you to ask if your intro was helpful

Ten days after you make an intro, we’ll send you an email reminder. Simply reply to this with a quick “Hey, how did it go?” style message. Your message goes directly to the person you made the intro for.

2. Replies delivered to your inbox

When they receive your message, they can simply reply in the email. Their comment will be emailed straight back to you. Your Intros account will also store a record.

3. Let the connection develop, then check in again

Six months later, we’ll send you another reminder like this. You can turn these reminders on or off at any time.

INSIGHTS INTO YOUR NETWORK

When you record an intro, we’ll send you a quick confirmation email, which includes information on how many intros you’ve already made for, and received from, the people involved.

This isn’t about keeping score. Sometimes we simply don’t notice how much someone is doing to help us. So we forget to help out the people who matter most. Why not try it now?

You can also view lots of insights about your intros and your contacts in our brand new dashboard. https://intros.to/dashboard
